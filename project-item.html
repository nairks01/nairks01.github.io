Mobile App for ER Wait Times

IOS App that displays wait times for emergency rooms in Richmond, VA

I made this app in the summer after 9th grade as a personal project. I decided to make an app because I was interested in programming and wanted to learn more about it. My inspiration was that there wasn't a tool like this at the time and I thought it would be very beneficial if people could have quick access to ER wait times.

Throughout the completion of this project, I learned the basics of Swift and XCode. I also learned fundamental UI and UX design aspects and gained a better understanding of the importance of text sizes, fonts, transitions, button placements, and overall intuitiveness of the app.
